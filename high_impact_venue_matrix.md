## Practical Recommendation

Build and submit the current version for **Energy Conversion and Management: X**
first. Every section should answer an energy-management decision question:

- Which scenarios can be screened out before AC corrective optimization?
- How many risky scenarios and bus-level violations are missed?
- How does topology/PV/loading-conditioned conformal calibration trade interval
  width against violation recall?
- How does the screening layer support renewable hosting without claiming to
  replace OPF/MPC?

Pivot to **Energy and AI** if the final cover letter and introduction emphasize
trustworthy AI more than energy conversion/management. Treat **Advances in
Applied Energy**, **Applied Energy**, and **IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid** as
upgrade routes requiring additional external feeder validation and a stronger
operational benchmark.
